## Full Description

### English

PixelBridge makes it effortless to install the AppLovin Axon Pixel on your Shopline store — no coding required.

**Why Axon Pixel?**
AppLovin's Axon Pixel enables precise ad attribution, helping you measure and optimize your Return on Ad Spend (ROAS) across AppLovin's advertising network.

**Key Features:**
- One-click installation — pixel is auto-injected into your store
- Tracks all key e-commerce events: page views, add-to-cart, checkout, and purchases
- Real-time event statistics dashboard
- SHA-256 hashed user data for privacy compliance
- Purchase deduplication to ensure accurate attribution
- Multi-layer add-to-cart detection (fetch/XHR interception, Shopline Analytics API, click listeners)
- Zero impact on store performance (async loading)

**How it works:**
1. Install the app and enter your AppLovin Axon Pixel Key
2. The pixel is automatically added to every page of your store
3. E-commerce events are tracked and sent to AppLovin for attribution
4. View aggregate statistics in your dashboard

**Pricing:**
$1.99/month with a 3-day free trial. Cancel anytime.

## FAQ

### English

**Q: Where do I find my AppLovin Axon Pixel Key?**
A: Log in to your AppLovin MAX Dashboard, go to Account → Keys. Your Pixel Key is listed there.

**Q: Do I need to add any code to my store?**
A: No. Once you install the app and enter your Pixel Key, the tracking script is automatically injected into every page of your store.

**Q: What events does the pixel track?**
A: The pixel tracks page views, product views, add-to-cart, begin checkout, and purchase events — all the key conversion events needed for ad attribution.

**Q: Will the pixel slow down my store?**
A: No. The pixel loads asynchronously and has zero impact on your store's page load speed.

**Q: What happens after my 3-day free trial ends?**
A: Your subscription will automatically convert to $1.99/month. You can cancel anytime from the Shopline app management page.

**Q: How do I uninstall the app?**
A: Go to your Shopline admin → Apps → find PixelBridge → Uninstall. All your data will be automatically deleted.

**Q: Can I see how many events are being tracked?**
A: Yes. The dashboard shows real-time aggregate statistics including page views, add-to-cart, checkout, and purchase counts, along with total GMV.
